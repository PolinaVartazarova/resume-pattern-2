Jon Snow

Birth date: 15.08.1986

Birth place: Winterfell, Westeros


Jon Snow is a fictional character in the A Song of Ice and Fire
series of fantasy novels by American author George R. R. Martin,
and its television adaptation Game of Thrones, in which he is
portrayed by English actor Kit Harington.
He is one of the most popular characters in the series, and The
New York Times cites him as one of the author's finest creations.

Jon is described as having strong Stark features with a lean build,
long face, dark brown hair and dark grey eyes.
Jon has the surname "Snow" (customarily used for illegitimate
noble children in the North) and is resented by Ned's wife Catelyn, who views him as a constant reminder of Ned's infidelity.
